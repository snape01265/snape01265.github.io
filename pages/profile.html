Backstory

Hello! If you have stumbled onto this page, I figure I owe you a proper introduction.

I'm Sun and as the title above suggests, I make games for a living. I grew up playing just about everything — RPGs, platformers, strategy, party, shooters, etc — if it had a start screen, I was there. Somewhere along the way, I stopped seeing games as just entertainment and started appreciating them for what they really are: a unique blend of storytelling, visuals, music, and interactivity that no other medium can pull off quite the same way.

Naturally, I wanted to become a part of that process.

Now, with a few years of professional experience under my belt working across gameplay, tools, UI, animation, and other aspects, I've put this website together to show what I've been up to — and to see where the road takes me next. Feel free to poke around!
